# Troubleshooting Guide - Login Issues

## Common Login Problems and Solutions

### Problem: "Invalid email or password"

**Possible Causes:**
- Incorrect email address
- Wrong password
- Caps Lock is enabled
- Account doesn't exist

**Solutions:**
1. Double-check your email address for typos
2. Make sure Caps Lock is off
3. Try resetting your password using "Forgot Password"
4. Verify you're using the correct email address associated with your account

### Problem: "Account locked" or "Too many login attempts"

**Cause:** Multiple failed login attempts triggered security lockout

**Solution:**
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset your password
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Problem: Can't receive password reset email

**Possible Causes:**
- Email went to spam folder
- Wrong email address entered
- Email provider blocking our emails

**Solutions:**
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add noreply@company.com to your contacts
3. Wait 5-10 minutes and check again
4. Try requesting a new reset email
5. Contact your email provider if emails are being blocked

### Problem: "Email not verified"

**Cause:** Account created but verification email not clicked

**Solution:**
1. Check your email for the verification message
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. Request a new verification email from the login page if needed
4. Check spam folder if email isn't in inbox

### Problem: Two-factor authentication not working

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure your device time is synchronized correctly
2. Check that you're entering the 6-digit code correctly
3. Use a backup code if available
4. Disable and re-enable 2FA if problems persist
5. Contact support for account recovery

### Problem: Social login (Google/GitHub) not working

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure you're using the same email for both accounts
2. Clear your browser cookies and cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check if your social account email matches your account email
5. Try logging in with email/password instead

## Still Having Issues?

If none of these solutions work:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser or incognito mode
3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
4. Contact our support team with:
   - Your email address
   - Error message you're seeing
   - Browser and device information
